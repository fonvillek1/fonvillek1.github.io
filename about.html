The Art Of Cooking

Enjoying Food Prepared By You.

About

Learning the art of cooking so that you are able to prepare and enjoy healthy meals. It is all about loving what you do because when this happens it will never be considered work. This is how we will teach you how to cook, we make sure that the environment is peaceful with no stress. Our aim is foster the joy of cooking that will transcend once you leave our kitchen.

Before beginning we will talk about the ins and outs of being in a kitchen. Remember size doesn't matter, the only thing we are concerned about is your safety first and foremost, then we will talk about utensils, seasonsings, bowls, pots, pans, olive oil, coconut oil and non-dairy butters. It should also be noted that we will begin our first cooking demonstration being conducted by one of our chefs, she will explain what is going to be cooked, the health benefits and the different ways the meal can be enjoyed with other foods.
