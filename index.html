Our Mission

It is our mission for everyone to eat healthy and lead more active lifestyles. We have seen a rise of such unhealthiness within our country specifically with the underserved and unrepresented. We realize due to the lack of knowledge and also resources. Our goal is to provide opportunities to not only educate about the importance of eating healthy but to teach how to prepare meals that will not only help you in the long run but also to be able to teach others. We are also able to provide food vouchers so that you can continue what you have learned in class and also metrocards to those who need it.

We Are Funded By

Fonville Living would not be possible if not for our amazing sponsors: The Obama Foundation, Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation and OneUnited Bank. When we began pitching our idea about this need that we believed was sorely lacking for the underserved and underrepresented, it resignated with those who are now our partners. They understood the need for healthy nutrition because then that will mean people can be more productive and live healthier.
