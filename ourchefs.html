Our Chefs

Our Chefs who will be teaching you how to prepare your own healthy meals for you and your family. Our chefs were hand picked not only for their broad expertise of the culinary arts but they are just as passion driven to teach others not only how to throw down in the kitchen and cook amazing meals but also how to live healthy all while doing it. Our incredible chefs hail from all over the country and we can not wait until you meet them. But first let me introduce you to our amazing crew. They are; Kani Greene, Danai Leland, Michonne Reede, Diamond Swahili, Evelyn Walters and Makayla Williams.

These incredible women have won culinary awards from all over the world and have also worn the hat of Top Chef. We are incredibly excited and honored that they have joined our cause and team. We are going to have a great time in the kitchen with these fabulous women. For whomever will like to participate, at the end of the cooking module we will have our very own top chef competition. Complete with prizes and a trophy.
